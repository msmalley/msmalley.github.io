Introducing The Pandora Collection

Artists born on the blockchains generating on-chain art

 

 

Non-Fungible Artists are represented as ERC721 NFTs and each have one of three potential artistic styles that they use to generate new NFT artwork, all of which is stored entirely on-chain.

Each artist is limited to creating upto 26 unique artworks, so as the universal price of minting new art increases with each piece that is generated, artists are worth more if they don't mint art.

 

Since an artist must have two unique initials as their identifiers there are only 676 potential artists available, which results in a total of up-to only 17,576 unique artworks.
